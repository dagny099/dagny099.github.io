My Journey

From MIT lab benches to enterprise boardrooms, the path of making data make sense to real people. This page maps the connections — people, tools, and ideas — that have shaped how I think and build.

The Path: From Mind to Machine to Meaning

2003-2010

The Academic Foundation

Dual BS in Electrical Engineering and Biology at UT Austin, followed by PhD in Cognitive Science at MIT. Studied how humans process visual information across 900+ natural scenes—work that still influences how I approach data visualization and user experience design.

2010-2015

The Translation Years

Transitioned from academia to applied research at UT Austin's HABLA lab, building data pipelines for bilingual language studies. Discovered my passion for making complex data accessible to non-technical researchers.

2015-2017

The Entrepreneurial Leap

Co-founded BALEX Healthcare Services, wearing every hat from compliance officer to software developer. Learned firsthand how data flows through real businesses and the critical importance of making it actionable for decision-makers.

2017-2021

The Consulting Evolution

Consulted across healthcare and software industries, specializing in digital transformation. Developed expertise in translating between technical teams and business stakeholders—essentially becoming a data interpreter.

2021-Present

The Data Science Synthesis

Evolved into a full-stack data scientist, combining cognitive science insights with modern ML/AI tools. Focus on creating data products that not only work technically but actually make sense to the humans who use them.

Core Throughlines

Three principles that have guided every career transition:

Understanding the User
Whether studying human visual attention or designing dashboards, I start with how people actually process information
Building Bridges
From translating between psychology and computer science to connecting technical teams with business needs
Making Data Actionable
Data is only valuable when it leads to better decisions. My mission is turning insights into action.
